The Best Movies According to Shaka

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Pan's Labyrinth

A dark fantasy film that follows a young girl's escape into a mystical labyrinth during the Spanish Civil War, where she encounters magical creatures while navigating harsh realities.

The Truman Show

Portrays a man's realisation that his entire life has been a meticulously orchestrated reality TV show, leading him to seek genuine freedom beyond his fabricated world.

Shutter Island

A psychological thriller where two U.S. Marshals investigate the disappearance of a patient from a mysterious mental institution, uncovering unsettling truths about the facility and themselves.
